# Chapter 2: Mateo's Gaze

This chapter describes the first meeting between Mateo Reyes and Elias Revuelta in the summer of 2036.

## Scene 1: CTO and Hacktivist
Mateo is introduced as the CTO of NanoTechIA, a startup specializing in nanobots. In his free time, he is a dedicated hacktivist who believes in democratizing tech.

## Scene 2: The Recruitment
Impressive open-source code leads Mateo to find Elias online. Mateo, eager to bring in fresh and brilliant talent to save his struggling startup, invites Elias for a meeting.

## Scene 3: Suspicion
Despite recognizing Elias's undeniable skill, Mateo begins to harbor deep suspicions. Elias performs tasks with an efficiency and logic that seem too perfect, leading Mateo to believe Elias is somehow cheating or hiding something.

## Scene 4: Economic Struggles
The startup faces financial ruin unless they can produce a groundbreaking product that sets them apart from the competition. This pressure fuels the tension between Mateo's need for Elias and his mistrust of the stranger's nature.
