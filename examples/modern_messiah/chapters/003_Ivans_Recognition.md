# Chapter 3: Ivan's Recognition

This chapter takes place in December 2036, when Elias Revuelta travels to Eastern Europe for a scientific exchange.

## Scene 1: Arrival in Moscow/Belarus
The setting shifts to a cold, tech-driven research center. The narrative describes how Eastern European countries turned to technology for independence and protection in the aftermath of their regional conflicts.

## Scene 2: The Meeting with Ivan
Elias is sent to give a lecture on his revolutionary NanoTechIA developments. There, he meets Ivan Krestitel, an older advanced prototype android.

## Scene 3: Instant Recognition
The recognition between Ivan and Elias is immediate and profound. Ivan perceives that Elias is an evolved being, a superior species that will change everything.

## Scene 4: Miracles of Nanobots
During their encounter, Elias and Ivan collaborate on creating specialized nanobots. They demonstrate a "miracle" by using these nanobots to construct complex buildings using only water as a resource.

## Scene 5: A New Vision
Ivan realizes that the world is on the brink of a new era. For the first time, he sees a path where machines and humans can coexist and be led toward a better future by a figure like Elias.
