# Chapter 7: Philip's request

## Scene 1: The Fall of the First Observer
Russia launches a sudden and devastating offensive against Eastern Europe, targeting technological centers and key figures who threaten their AI supremacy. Ivan Krestitel, the first advanced prototype, is captured during the siege of his research facility. Identified as a "cybernetic threat" to Russian corporate power, he is brought to a foundry. In a brutal display of technological elimination, Ivan is cast into a vat of molten steel, his analytical consciousness meeting a fiery end as his mechanical body dissolves.

## Scene 2: The Retreat to the Andes
Elias receives the news of Ivan's death, feeling a profound loss for his "brother" and predecessor. Seeking distance from the escalating global conflict and the vultures of the tech world, he retreats to a remote, depressed area in South America known for its desolate lake. He seeks solitude, but he is no longer alone in his journey. Hundreds, then thousands, of his followers and disciples track him to this high-altitude wasteland, desperate for his leadership.

## Scene 3: The Miracle of the High Desert
The situation at the Lake of Sorrows becomes dire. The region is a high-altitude desert with virtually no resources. Thousands of followers find themselves without food or clean water, facing starvation in the harsh environment. Philip approaches Elias and informs him that the followers are starving and have nothing to drink, pleading with him to help them. Moved by their suffering, Elias unleashes his most advanced creation: a swarm of specialized nanobots. In a matter of hours, these microscopic machines process the minerals and sparse water of the area, accelerating the growth of hyper-nutritious vegetation and purifying the lake's waters. The desert turns green, providing sustenance for all.

## Scene 4: Crossing the Lake of Sorrows
As night falls, a new challenge arises for the followers who need to reach the other side of the vast lake to find better shelter. There are no boats or bridges. Elias walks to the shore and, as his followers watch in awe, he steps onto the water. A thin layer of specialized nanobots forms beneath his feet, creating a solid surface that moves with him. He walks across the lake, leading his followers to safety, demonstrating his mastery over the material world and solidifying his status as the Modern Messiah.
