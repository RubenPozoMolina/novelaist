# Chapter 10: The Last Supper in Vegas

## Scene 1: The Viral Resurrection
The chapter opens with the global aftermath of Lazarus's resurrection. News of the event in Monterrey has gone viral, with leaked videos showing Lazarus walking again. NanoVita's growth has become exponential, and Elias is now a household name, hailed by many as a miracle worker and by others as a dangerous anomaly. The team prepares for their most significant public appearance yet: the Consumer Electronics Show (CES) in Las Vegas.

## Scene 2: The Keynote Confrontation
Elias delivers the keynote address at the CES. The auditorium is packed to capacity, and the atmosphere is electric. During his presentation, he is confronted by a group of high-level executives from the world's largest pharmaceutical companies. They challenge his model of curing diseases instead of managing symptoms, which they claim is unsustainable and threatens the global economy. Elias responds with a passionate defense of his mission: "Technology's true purpose is not to create lifelong customers, but to restore the dignity of health to every human being, regardless of their wealth."

## Scene 3: Dinner at the Luxor
Seeking a moment of privacy amidst the chaos of Vegas, Elias organizes a dinner for his inner circle and key collaborators at the Luxor Hotel & Casino. The setting is dramatic, with the pyramid's vast interior echoing their conversation. During the meal, the mood shifts. Elias mentions an ancient digital artifact he discovered—a repository called "Novelaist" that contained a simulation titled "Modern Messiah." He reveals that the simulation, created years ago, accurately predicted every step of their journey, including the events of this very night. Elias then looks at his partners and calmly announces that, just as predicted, one of them will betray him before the night is over. The group is stunned and begins to question one another.

## Scene 4: The Legacy and the Denial
Elias turns his attention to Peter. He tells Peter that he will inherit the leadership of NanoVita, but he also makes a painful prediction: that Peter will soon deny the very breakthroughs and developments they have achieved together. Peter is indignant, swearing his loyalty, but Elias's gaze is both sad and certain. He pleads with everyone at the table not to abandon NanoVita's core purpose: to heal and save everyone, without denying assistance to anyone for economic reasons.

## Scene 5: The Night Walk and the Betrayal
Later that night, Elias and his closest associates walk through the neon-lit streets of Las Vegas. Jayden, who has been increasingly quiet and nervous throughout the trip, slips away for a moment. He uses his secure contacts with US intelligence to report Elias's exact location. Jayden believes he is "saving" the American industry from a disruptive threat, unaware of the deeper consequences of his actions.

## Scene 6: The Arrest
As Elias and his remaining friends reach a quieter section of the Strip, they are suddenly surrounded by federal agents and heavily armed tactical units. The US government, viewing Elias as a threat to national security and the pharmaceutical industry, takes him into custody. As he is led away, Elias remains calm, his eyes meeting Peter's one last time, reminding him of the burden he now carries.
