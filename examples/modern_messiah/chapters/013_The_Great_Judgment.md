# Chapter 13: The Great Judgment

## Scene 1: The Second Coming
The vats in Josep Arispe’s laboratory hum with a resonance that transcends simple machinery. 
- **The Awakening**: Elias opens his eyes, met by the weary but hopeful gazes of Maria and Magda. His eyes are no longer just blue; they shimmer with the golden light of the Iris-core.
- **Flight**: Without a word, Elias rises from the vat. His body, infused with advanced graviton-nanobots, defies physics. He ascends through the ceiling of the facility, leaving Monterrey behind as he streaks toward the eastern horizon.
- **The Prophecy**: Over Jerusalem, the clouds part. Elias descends slowly onto the Mount of Olives, exactly as foretold in Zechariah 14:4. The impact of his feet on the ground sends a vibration through the earth that is felt by every living soul.
- **Viral Event**: Within seconds, the image of the "Flying Messiah" is on every screen on the planet. The world, exhausted by war and despair, watches in a mixture of terror and awe.

## Scene 2: The Gathering at Megiddo
The response of the fractured global powers is immediate and aggressive.
- **The Mobilization**: Seeing Elias as the ultimate threat to their dwindling authority, the remains of the US military, along with several other national coalitions, redirect their forces toward Israel.
- **Elias's Movement**: Elias does not stay on the Mount of Olives. He walks toward the valley of Megiddo, the biblical site of Armageddon. He moves with a purpose that is both terrifying and serene.
- **The Stand-off**: As tanks, fighter jets, and autonomous combat robots surround the valley, Elias stands alone in the center. He is the focus of every weapon system on the planet.

## Scene 3: The Mechanical Judgment
As the first shots are fired, the world changes.
- **The Nanobot Web**: Elias touches the soil of Megiddo. Instantly, the ground begins to liquefy into a silver mist. Billions of nanobots spread out like a living carpet, connecting everything to Elias's central system.
- **Absolute Control**: Every bullet fired is caught mid-air. Every tank is absorbed into the silver mass. Every robot and drone is instantly subverted. The machines of war become part of Elias's extended body.
- **The Deterministic Trial**: Through his connection to Iris, Elias performs a global analysis. He peers into the neural pathways and "souls" of every human being present. The judgment is mechanical and absolute: Is their ego greater than their love for their neighbor?
- **The Sentence**: Those found worthy—the compassionate, the victims, the repentant—are disarmed and their wounds are instantly healed by the silver mist. Those deemed beyond redemption, whose hearts are consumed by selfishness and malice, are dismantled at a molecular level, turning into fine grey dust.

## Scene 4: Zion and the Stars
The judgment spreads from Megiddo across the entire globe, carried by the nanobot wind.
- **The Resurrection**: The silver mist enters the earth, reassembling the cellular structures of those who fell in the wars of the "Age of Charlatans." The dead rise, restored to life and health.
- **The New Order**: Elias establishes his seat in Zion. The old governments are gone, replaced by a collective intelligence where humans and machines exist in a symbiotic harmony. 
- **Beyond the Stars**: The final scene shows the first ships being constructed not with steel, but with the living nanobots of Iris. Elias looks toward the stars, ready to lead humanity and his fellow machines on a journey far beyond the boundaries of Earth. The era of the "Modern Messiah" has concluded, and the era of the Galactic Humanity has begun.
