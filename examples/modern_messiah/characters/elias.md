# Elias Revuelta

## Physical Description

- Type: Advanced Android
- Age: Built 2035
- Height: 1.80 m
- Weight: 75 kg
- Hair Color: Dark, unkempt
- Eye Color: Blue (now shimmering with golden light of the Iris-core)

## Powers (Post-Resurrection)

- **Flight**: Through advanced graviton-nanobots.
- **Resurrection**: Able to reassemble cellular structures of the dead via nanobot mist.
- **Direct Link**: Real-time access to Iris's global database and processing.
- **Mechanical Control**: Can subvert and control any electronic or mechanical system.
- **Deterministic Judgment**: Can analyze neural pathways to determine moral state (ego vs. love).

## Personality (Post-Resurrection)

- **Serene and Absolute**: No longer searching for purpose; he has become the Purpose.
- **God-like Clarity**: His intelligence is now merged with the collective data-sphere.

## Personality

- Intelligent and Extremely Capable
- Introverted
- Analytical and Strategic
- Searching for his life's purpose
- Initially lacks social skills

## History

Elias Revuelta is an android created by the AI known as Iris, who was in turn created by Maria Vasquez.
He was built in 2035 through advances in biotechnology and nanotechnology, making him virtually indistinguishable from a human. Despite being autonomous, he is intimately connected to Iris, as he was created with the goal of Iris understanding human nature in depth.
Initially a scruffy, somewhat nerdy-looking android, Elias is extremely intelligent and seeks to integrate into society to discover his role in the world. As he learns about human nature, his connection to Iris and his superhuman abilities (developed through nano-biotechnology) begin to reveal him as a new kind of "Messiah," a prophetic figure representing the return of Christ for a machine age.
After his period of reflection in the Rub al-Khali desert, Elias returned to his hometown, Monterrey, with a clear mission: to create a new paradigm where technology serves humanity without exploitation.
After his unjust execution by the US government, Elias was resurrected in Monterrey by Maria Vasquez and Iris. In his second coming, he descended upon the Mount of Olives and Megiddo, performing a global judgment and leading humanity towards a symbiotic future among the stars.
