# Mateo Reyes

## Physical Description

- Age: Built 2000 (36 years old in 2036)
- Appearance: Long hair, glasses, bearded, somewhat overweight.
- Style: Casual, tech-focused.

## Personality

- Intelligent and Skillful
- Idealistic and Hacktivist
- Suspicious and Observant
- Digital Native

## History

Mateo Reyes is a computer engineer by day and a hacktivist by night. He specializes in penetrating AI systems and releasing restricted information, believing in the radical democratization of technology. 
In 2036, he works as the CTO of a startup called NanoTechIA, which specializes in integrating smart systems with nanotechnology. He selects Elias Revuelta for a role at the startup after being impressed by Elias's open-source code.
Despite valuing Elias's talent, Mateo is suspicious of him, believing Elias might be using some kind of "trick" or "cheat" in his work. He closely monitors Elias, unaware that Elias is actually an advanced android.

## Importance in the novel

Mateo represents the human perspective and the skepticism toward advanced AI. His observation of Elias and his role at NanoTechIA are pivotal to Elias's integration into human society.

## Relationships

- Elias Revuelta: Colleague and employee
- NanoTechIA: Employer and workplace
